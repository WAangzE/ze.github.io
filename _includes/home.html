Biography

I am currently a RecSys architect at ByteDance. My research spans broad areas of computer systems, including distributed systems, streaming systems and machine learning systems. We are building the state-of-art system architect in the world, exploring the combination of academic and industry to address the real-world problems.

My advisor was Prof. Hongtao Lu at CSE Department. I was once a member of Tencent AI Lab and Tencent Youtu Lab.

Research

Distributed Machine Learning

Distributed machine learning is a cross-field topic of theories, algorithms and systems. It covers many part of computer science, including data, model, comunication and hardware. With the help of distributed machine learning, you can train and serve large scale machine learning models, which is very essential especially in large IT companies. For myself, most of my efforts has been devoted to the Parameter Server architect and the distributed machine learning system based on data stream.

Streaming System

Processing large-scale data is a significant part of large systems. Streaming sytem allows businesses to crave even-more timely insights into their data. Also, it is a perfect way of addressing the never-ending, unbounded datasets and make computation stress more evenly distributed according to time. With engines like Flink and Spark, you will be able to explore the funny part of this topic.
